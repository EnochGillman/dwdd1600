Home

Welcome to Raster Roster, a collection of resources aimed at teaching the fundamentals to 3D graphics.

What you will learn

In these tutorials, you will explore the fundamental concepts that allow us to send pixels to the screen and create a 3D image. This website will delve into rasterization, the most popular technique for real-time applications since the late 70's. Even in the advent of ray tracing, rasterization remains the prefered rendering method for it's speed and simplicity.

Even if you later become interested in ray tracing, it is generally agreed upon that rasterization is the better launch-pad for learning 3D graphics. The past several decades have given graphics programmers time to design intuitive interfaces for rasterization, where ray tracing has not been widely adopted until recently.
