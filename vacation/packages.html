Baja California: Experience Nature's Wonder

Stop by the local blowhole!

La Bufadora is not your typical marine geyser. Instead of erupting due to thermal energy, air pressure builds up in a sea cave as waves rise. When they receed, this air is released, leading to seawater making an explosive leap upwards. This package offers the means to arrive at this marvelous spot. Be sure to visit the blowhole if you're in town!

  • Transport: Cali Bus Tours
  • Hotel: Cuatro Cuatros

$100
